Privacy Policy

App: Health Reports

Developer: Iniaki Boudiaf

Effective Date: March 29, 2026

1. Overview

Health Reports is a free iOS app that generates PDF health reports from Apple Health data. This privacy policy explains what data the app accesses, how it's used, and your rights.

2. Data We Access

The app requests read-only access to the following Apple Health categories:

CategoryExamples
ActivitySteps, distance, exercise minutes, flights climbed, calories
HeartHeart rate, resting HR, HRV, blood pressure, VO2 Max
SleepSleep duration and stage breakdown (deep, REM, core, awake)
BodyWeight, BMI, body fat percentage, height
RespiratoryRespiratory rate, blood oxygen saturation
NutritionMacronutrients, vitamins, minerals, water, caffeine
MobilityWalking speed, step length, double support time
RunningRunning power, stride length, ground contact time
WorkoutsActivity type, duration, calories burned, distance
Lab & ClinicalBlood glucose, insulin delivery
OtherHearing levels, reproductive health metrics

The app never writes to Apple Health. Access is read-only.

3. How Your Data Is Used

Your health data is used for one purpose only: generating PDF reports on your device.

  • Data is read from Apple HealthKit
  • Data is aggregated (averages, min/max, trends) on your device
  • A PDF report is rendered on your device
  • The PDF is saved locally to your device's storage
  • You choose when and how to share the PDF (AirDrop, email, etc.)

4. Data Storage

  • Report metadata (title, date range, selected metrics) is stored locally using SwiftData
  • PDF files are stored in the app's sandboxed Documents directory
  • User preferences (name, default report title) are stored locally via UserDefaults
  • All stored data can be deleted from the app's Settings screen

5. What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not transmit health data to any server, cloud service, or third party
  • We do not collect analytics or usage telemetry
  • We do not use any third-party SDKs, trackers, or advertising frameworks
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize your data in any way
  • We do not require an account or login
  • We do not use cookies or web tracking
  • The app has no backend — there is no server to send data to

6. Third-Party Services

The app contains a single external link to "Buy Me a Coffee" for optional donations. This link opens in your browser and is subject to Buy Me a Coffee's own privacy policy. No data is shared with them from the app.

7. Data Sharing

The app does not share your data automatically. You control all sharing:

  • You choose to generate a report
  • You choose which metrics to include
  • You choose to share the PDF via the iOS share sheet
  • The recipient and method are entirely your choice

8. Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The app accesses only data already stored in Apple Health by the user or their devices.

9. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Health data is read on-demand and not cached beyond the current report generation
  • Saved reports (PDFs) persist until you delete them
  • You can delete all saved reports and preferences from Settings > Clear All Data
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data

10. Security

  • All data processing occurs on-device within iOS's security sandbox
  • No network connections are made for data processing
  • PDFs are stored in the app's private sandboxed directory
  • Apple's HealthKit permission system controls which data the app can access

11. Your Rights

You can:

  • Revoke HealthKit access at any time via iOS Settings > Health > Data Access
  • Delete all saved reports from within the app
  • Delete the app entirely to remove all associated data

12. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the effective date at the top will change. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
